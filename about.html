We're a team of HR professionals, payroll specialists, and technology writers dedicated to helping businesses make smarter software decisions.

Our Mission

HR and payroll software is one of the most important investments any business makes — yet the landscape is confusing, vendor-driven, and full of jargon. We exist to cut through that noise.

Social HR Software is an independent publication. We receive no sponsorships or payment from software vendors. Every article is written from the perspective of HR professionals who have actually implemented and used these systems.

Our goal is simple: help you make better decisions about the tools that power your people operations.
